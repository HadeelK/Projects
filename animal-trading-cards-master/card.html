Tigers

After a century of decline, overall wild tiger numbers are starting to tick upward. Based on the best available information, tiger populations are stable or increasing.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Population: Around 3,900
  • Weight: 220–660 pounds
  • Length: 6–10 feet
